Instructions:

The aim of the game is to destroy the incoming meteors before they reach the base. Destroying a meteor will earn you 50 points but missing a meteor will lose you 50 points and 10% from your defence.

In order to win a level you must have 1 point or more and have a defence level of over 1%, getting a defence level of under 1% will instantly lose you the level.
